About Us

Top of Mind was created by professional accountants, for professional accountants.

Starting a business may not always have been top of mind for us, but it was certainly at the back of it. With a collective 15 years' experience in the industry and a background in teaching at some of Canada's most prestigious universities, we started Top of Mind so that we could empower you.

Are you an enterprise? You deserve to have the flexibility to acquire the talent you need, whenever you need it. We know that each need can be different, requiring a nuanced set of skills. We want to empower you to find them.

Are you an accounting professional? You deserve to have the flexibility to choose your work, and to develop your skills with focused intention, so they can better serve your goals. We want to empower you to chart your own course.

Whether you're a firm, a business or an accounting professional, we know that you have your own unique needs. That's why we're redefining flexibility, so you can find just the right fit.
